Adopt Me

Finding your soulmate is easy.

We’ve streamlined the adoption process to ensure every pet goes to a safe, loving, and permanent home.

1.Find Your Perfect Match

Browse our available pets. Use our filters to find a companion that matches your energy levels, living situation, and family dynamic.

  1. 1. Filter by age or breed
  2. 2. Read detailed personality bios

2. The Application & Chat

Once you find "the one," submit a simple application. Our counselors will reach out for a friendly chat to discuss the pet's specific needs and answer any questions you have.

3. The Meet & Greet

Chemistry matters! We arrange a session at our sanctuary (or a neutral park) for you to play, walk, and bond with your potential new family member.

4. Welcome Home

Finalize the paperwork, pay the adoption fee (which covers all medicals), and head home! We’ll provide a "New Parent Starter Guide" to help with the first 48 hours.
